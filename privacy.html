Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

iRTSP does not collect any data. It has no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers of its own. Nothing you capture or stream ever reaches the developer or any third party.

What iRTSP does

iRTSP runs a standard RTSP camera server on your device. Your camera, microphone, and — if you enable them — motion (IMU), location, altitude, LiDAR depth, and ARKit pose data are transmitted only over your own local network (Wi-Fi or hotspot) to players you choose to connect, such as VLC, ffmpeg, or OBS. This is a direct, peer-to-peer, real-time stream.

Data collection

The developer does not collect, store, transmit to any server, sell, or share any personal data or usage data. iRTSP contains no analytics or advertising SDKs. Because there is no cloud component, there is nothing for the developer to access.

Device permissions

iRTSP asks for permissions only to provide its features, and only while you use them:

  • Camera & Microphone — to capture the video and audio you stream.
  • Local Network — to serve the RTSP stream and advertise it via Bonjour on your LAN.
  • Location (optional) — only if you enable the GPS/heading channel; used to include location in your stream. It is not stored or sent anywhere by the developer.
  • Motion (optional) — only if you enable the IMU/altitude channel.

All of this data stays on your local network and is never retained by the developer.

Children's privacy

iRTSP is rated 4+ and does not collect data from anyone, including children.
