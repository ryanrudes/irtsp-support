Common questions

  • How do I view the stream?Tap Record, then point any RTSP player (VLC, ffmpeg, OBS) on the same Wi-Fi or hotspot at the rtsp://… URL the app shows (or scan its QR code).
  • Nothing connects.Make sure the player and iPhone are on the same network, and that Local Network access is allowed for iRTSP in Settings → Privacy & Security → Local Network.
  • How do I read the IMU / GPS / depth / pose data?The optional sensor channels are advertised over Bonjour on separate ports alongside the video. They're opt-in and off by default.
  • Does my footage go to the cloud?No. iRTSP streams peer-to-peer on your local network only. It has no account, no cloud, and collects no data. See the privacy policy.
